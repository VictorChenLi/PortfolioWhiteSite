Hello There!

I have 7 years work experience in software engineering(Android, React, NodeJs, etc) and 4+ years work experience in User experience design for multiple different companies from the different industry.

I'm an advocate of lean UX, which means I love agile and human center design. My design philosophy is rational, consistency and agile. Rational means all the design I come up with always based on certain reason, either from user testing or widely accepted design principle and patterns. Consistency means I will use style guideline and well-defined design principle across the whole design life cycle, which will reduce engineering effort and handoff time. Agile means I will perform design ideation, feasibility check, wireframe, prototyping, quick validation within each development cycle.


I think I can make a contribution to your project and help you make a great product.
